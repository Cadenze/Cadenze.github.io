# About me

Hi, I'm Boris, a PhD candidate in Pure Mathematics at the University of Waterloo.
I am studying algebra and algebraic geometry
under Drs. Jason Bell and Ben Webster.
I am currently working on a problem in non-commutative algebra,
which aims to help us better understand filtered deformations over domains.

Previously, I obtained my MMath in the same department;
and before that, I studied physics and mathematics
during my BSc at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver.
My [curriculum vitae](files/cv.pdf) is linked here.

In my free time, I like to play bridge (the card game), play the violin,
and very occasionally compose classical music.

(or at the top if you are on mobile)
for more information.
